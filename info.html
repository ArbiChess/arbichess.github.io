About Us

We are a group of loyal holders of ETH and supporters of the Arbitrum ecosystem.

Our project ArbiChess code will be completely forked from Tranchess.

We pay tribute to the Tranchess development team and thank them for creating such an excellent project. We will continue to follow their development trajectory.

Our code is all open-source, any review from the community is welcome and any problems found can get token rewards.

Zero VC/PE investments, no pre-mining or reserve.

To fit in the Arbitrum ecology, we set the tracing token to ETH at the code level and change 5% of seed investors to seven-day seed round staking mining at the economic model level. A total of 6 pools for the seven-day seed round staking mining.



Please note: This is a social experiment. It is recommended to participate in the experiment with a small amount of ETH.
